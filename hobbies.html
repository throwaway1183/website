Walking

I walk everyday, from hostel to lecture hall, then back to hostel and again towards the lecture hall and alas to my hostel. On my days off im walking in the corridors visiting other people or going outside to have food.
Walking is best enjoyed when you are jobless and have no work to do, it becomes stressful once you have somewhere to be and your time is limited.
It costs nothing to walk, considering you have working legs, and it keeps you healthy both in mind and body. In a world where our lives are becoming more and more sedentary, walking helps us live healthier lives.
